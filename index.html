Winner-Take-All; How We All Lose

April 2018

My friend and I created a project called Winner-Take-All; How We All Lose to speak out against single-member districts. Under our current system it is possible that a Congressional candidate could win a single-member seat with 51% of the vote, giving 100% of the representation to only 51% of the public. The other 49% have now been shut out of Congress. The solution we proposed was to create larger multi-member districts. A district would have multiple representatives so at least one of the representatives would more closely satisfy the views of any given voter in the electorate. Our project was selected for the Civics Fair State Finals and won third place.
